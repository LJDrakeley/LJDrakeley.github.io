About Me

Hobbies: Photography, camping & driving!

Pronouns: he/him/his

Hi! My name is Lewis Drakeley and I grew up in Wales, UK. I'm a huge fan of the countryside, and my favorite place in the world is the Mach Loop. I recently graduated from the University of Rochester where I followed my passion for physics and astronomy, and even took classes in psychology and American Sign Language. Here, I conducted research using the Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument (DESI) dataset, and even joined the university's rowing team. I hope to continue my journey in the United States and someday visit the Grand Canyon.
